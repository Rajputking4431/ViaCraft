## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft operates as a multi-vendor marketplace facilitator for resin handmade gifts, home decor, jewellery, and preservation products. 

This Shipping Policy provides information regarding the processing, shipping, tracking, and delivery of orders purchased on our Platform [Website URL].

## 2. Purpose
This policy ensures transparency regarding delivery timelines, shipping methods (manual shipping vs. integrated Shiprocket services), shipping costs, and customer/vendor expectations for order fulfillment.

## 3. Shipping & Facilitation Model
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a facilitation platform. We do not stock inventory or ship items ourselves. All orders are packed and dispatched directly by the independent Vendors from their respective business addresses.

## 4. Shipping Methods
Vendors on ViaCraft can utilize two main shipping models for order fulfillment:

### 4.1. Shiprocket Integrated Shipping
*   **Process:** The Vendor coordinates shipment generation directly through our integrated Shiprocket API on the Vendor Dashboard.
*   **Courier Partners:** Shiprocket automatically matches shipments with leading logistics partners in India (e.g., Delhivery, Blue Dart, FedEx India, Xpressbees, DTDC).
*   **Tracking:** Auto-generated tracking details are emailed to the Customer and updated in their Customer Dashboard.

### 4.2. Manual Shipping
*   **Process:** Vendors arrange their own courier partner independently and update tracking details manually on the Vendor Dashboard.
*   **Verification:** Vendors must input a valid tracking number and URL to confirm shipment dispatch.

## 5. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Delivery Address:** Customers must provide a complete and accurate delivery address, including postal code and contact number. ViaCraft or its Vendors will not be held responsible for delivery failures due to incorrect shipping addresses.
*   **Preservation Shipping:** For custom preservation orders (e.g., wedding flower preservation), the Customer is solely responsible for packaging and shipping the flowers or materials safely to the Vendor's workshop address at their own cost.

## 6. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Processing Time:** Vendors must package and ship orders within the processing time stated on their product listing page.
*   **Safe Packaging:** Since resin items can be fragile, Vendors must ensure adequate protective packaging (bubble wrap, sturdy boxes) is used.
*   **Dispatch Updates:** Vendors must mark the order as "Shipped" and provide tracking credentials within 24 hours of package handoff.

## 7. Delivery Timelines
*   **Standard Shipping:** Typically takes **5 to 10 business days** from the date of dispatch, depending on the destination (metros vs. tier 2/3 locations).
*   **Customized/Preservation Orders:** These items require additional manufacturing time (often 3 to 6 weeks) before dispatch. The delivery estimate starts after the creation process is complete.

## 8. Exceptions & Delays
*   **Remote Locations:** Shipments to remote areas, North-Eastern states, or Jammu & Kashmir may experience longer shipping durations.
*   **Force Majeure:** Delays due to natural disasters, strikes, public holidays, extreme weather, or courier hub backlogs are beyond the control of the Vendor and the Platform.

## 9. Lost or Damaged Shipments
*   **Damaged on Arrival:** If a product is damaged during transit, the Customer must notify Customer Support at [Support Email] with unboxing video/photo proof within 48 hours of delivery.
*   **Lost Shipments:** If a shipment is deemed lost by the courier partner, the Platform will coordinate with the Vendor to initiate a replacement order or a full refund.

## 10. Shipping Fees
Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on package weight, dimensions, origin address, and delivery pincode. These fees are paid by the Customer and passed to the Vendor (or utilized to pay integrated courier fees).

## 11. International Shipping
Currently, ViaCraft Vendors primarily ship within India. If a Vendor supports international shipping, additional duties, customs clearance charges, and international shipping rates will apply and must be borne by the Customer.

## 12. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not responsible for transit delays, courier handling errors, package loss, or transit damages. The logistics partner is strictly liable for shipment carriage, and the Vendor is responsible for appropriate packaging and dispatch validation.
