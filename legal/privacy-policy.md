## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft ("we," "our," "us," or "Platform"). ViaCraft is a multi-vendor online marketplace providing facilitation services for independent artisans selling resin handmade products, gifts, jewellery, home decor, preservation items, customized resin products, and handmade crafts ("Products") to buyers ("Customers" or "Users"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data we collect, process, or store.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you visit and use our website [Website URL] and its subdomains.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform Users (both Customers and Vendors) of our practices regarding data privacy, security, and our compliance with applicable laws, including the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. It aims to establish clear guidelines for details collected, stored on Supabase, and media assets processed via Cloudinary.

## 3. Definitions
*   **Platform:** ViaCraft, operating as an online marketplace facilitator.
*   **Vendor:** An independent third-party seller registered on the Platform to sell Products.
*   **Customer:** An individual who browses, purchases, or interacts with Vendor Products on the Platform.
*   **User:** Refers collectively to Customers, Vendors, and visitors.
*   **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

## 4. Information Collection
We collect several categories of information to provide and improve our services:

### 4.1. Account Information
*   **Customers:** Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing address, password, and order history.
*   **Vendors:** Full legal name, store name, phone number, business address, email address, PAN (Permanent Account Number), GSTIN (where applicable), bank account details (for settlement), and identity verification documents.

### 4.2. Media and Content Storage
*   **Cloudinary Storage:** Vendor product images, store banners, and related branding assets.
*   **Supabase Storage:** User profile images, invoice generation details, receipt documents, and transaction proof.

### 4.3. Technical and Usage Data
*   **Cookies & Analytics:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device details, pages viewed, time spent, and referral sources.
*   **Payment Information:** Payment-related actions are processed securely via independent payment gateways. The Platform does not store raw credit card numbers or banking passwords on its servers.

## 5. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Accuracy:** Customers must provide accurate, complete, and current information during account registration and order placement.
*   **Security:** Customers are responsible for keeping their account credentials confidential and notifying us immediately of unauthorized account access.

## 6. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Data Minimization:** Vendors must not use Customer personal data (disclosed for order fulfillment) for marketing or any other purpose without the Customer's explicit consent.
*   **Compliance:** Vendors are responsible for complying with local privacy regulations and must not store or leak Customer delivery information.

## 7. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Security Controls:** The Platform implements appropriate technical and organizational measures (such as SSL encryption, access control) to protect personal data stored on Supabase databases and Cloudinary media servers.
*   **Data Integrity:** The Platform processes user data solely as a facilitator to enable the transaction between Vendors and Customers.

## 8. Exceptions
We may disclose your Personal Data if required to do so:
*   By law, court order, or governmental authorities.
*   To detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.
*   To protect the rights, property, or safety of ViaCraft, our Users, or the public.

## 9. Limitation of Liability
The Platform shall not be liable for:
*   Any breach of privacy caused by security vulnerabilities in the Vendor's own networks, devices, or systems.
*   Direct, indirect, or incidental damages resulting from third-party payment gateway issues, or security breaches of external databases beyond the Platform's reasonable control.

## 10. User Rights
Under Indian data protection regulations, Users have the following rights:
*   **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your Personal Data held by the Platform.
*   **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete Personal Data.
*   **Right to Erasure (Account Deletion):** Request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal record-retention requirements (e.g. tax laws).

## 11. Third-Party Services
Our Platform integrates third-party services for payments, analytics, and asset storage. Please note that these services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to read their policies before providing personal details.

## 12. Children's Privacy
The Platform is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.
