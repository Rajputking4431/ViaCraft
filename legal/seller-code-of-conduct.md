## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft is a premium multi-vendor marketplace designed to connect buyers ("Customers") with independent artisans ("Vendors" or "Sellers") specializing in handmade resin crafts, jewelry, preservation items, and home decor.

To maintain the premium quality of [Website URL], all registered Vendors must adhere to this Seller Code of Conduct.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this Seller Code of Conduct is to establish clear operational, ethical, and quality standards for Vendors. Compliance is mandatory to ensure Customer trust, high ratings, and the overall integrity of the Platform.

## 3. Platform Facilitator Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a facilitation marketplace. The independent Vendor is solely responsible for inventory curation, product craftsmanship, quality control, order packaging, shipping compliance, and tax invoicing. The Platform monitors Vendor compliance to protect the ecosystem.

## 4. Key Seller Standards & Obligations

### 4.1. Product Authenticity & Quality
*   **True Handmade:** All items listed must be handcrafted, personalized, or custom-preservational resin and wood products. The listing of mass-produced, factory-made commercial goods is strictly prohibited.
*   **Material Quality:** Sellers must use non-toxic, high-grade epoxy resin, authentic dried flowers, treated wood, high-quality silicon, gold foil, and sturdy metals as described in listings.
*   **Accurate Photos:** Product images uploaded to Cloudinary must represent the actual item. Stock images from other websites are prohibited.

### 4.2. Operational & Shipping Excellence
*   **Timely Fulfillment:** Sellers must package and ship orders within the stated processing window.
*   **Robust Packaging:** Since resin, glass, and wood items can crack or break, Sellers must use bubble wrap, foam, and sturdy outer boxes. Damage caused by poor packaging is the Seller's sole liability.
*   **Tracking Updates:** Sellers must provide valid tracking links through the Vendor Dashboard immediately after dispatch.

### 4.3. Customer Communication & Support
*   **Professional Behavior:** Sellers must communicate politely, professionally, and promptly.
*   **Response Time:** Inquiries regarding custom specifications or order concerns must be answered within 24–48 hours.
*   **Review Ethics:** Sellers must not offer discounts, free gifts, or rebates in exchange for positive reviews, nor manipulate product ratings.

## 5. Prohibited Business Practices
Sellers on ViaCraft must avoid the following actions:
1.  **Fee Avoidance:** Initiating transactions through the Platform and persuading Customers to pay via external bank links or wallets to bypass the 8–10% platform commission.
2.  **Listing Counterfeit Goods:** Replicating copyright-protected images or selling items with trademarked logos without authorization.
3.  **Spamming:** Sending unsolicited messages, advertisements, or marketing materials to Customers.

## 6. Customer & Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Customer:** Customers must respect the craftsmanship process, provide clear requirements for custom items, and communicate respectfully.
*   **Vendor:** Vendors must honor their delivery promises, issue invoices with appropriate GST details, and handle refund disputes fairly.

## 7. Platform Rights & Enforcement
ViaCraft reserves the right to enforce this Code of Conduct. Non-compliant Sellers may face:
*   Temporary suspension of product listings.
*   Withholding of weekly settlements during active investigations.
*   Removal of Vendor badges or search ranking downgrades.
*   Permanent closure of the Vendor store and deletion of the account.
