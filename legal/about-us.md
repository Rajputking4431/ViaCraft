## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft ("Platform"). ViaCraft is a dedicated multi-vendor online marketplace designed to support independent artisans specializing in handmade resin crafts, personalized gifts, jewellery, home decor, and preservation art.

Through [Website URL], we connect passionate creators with customers looking for custom-tailored keepsakes.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this About Us page is to share our brand mission, vision, operational model, and story, and to outline the values that guide ViaCraft's marketplace ecosystem.

## 3. Marketplace Facilitator & Support Model
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Framework:** ViaCraft operates as an online marketplace facilitator. We provide the technology stack (React interfaces, Supabase database storage, Cloudinary media rendering, and secure checkout solutions) to empower individual sellers. Every purchase directly supports an independent artisan.

## 4. Our Mission
Our mission is to democratize the handcrafted resin art marketplace. We aim to:
*   Empower local artisans and small-scale creators by giving them access to digital storefronts, dashboards, and automated logistics.
*   Offer Customers a curated space to discover, personalize, and purchase bespoke gifts and memories.

## 5. Our Vision
To become India's leading destination for handmade crafts, custom resin preservation, and personalized gifting, defined by quality, trust, and community support.

## 6. Our Story
ViaCraft was born from a simple observation: handmade resin art requires extensive skill, patience, and attention to detail, yet local artists lack the tools to manage digital storefronts, logistics, payment gateways, and client communications. 

We built ViaCraft as a bridge—taking care of the technological and operational heavy lifting so our artisans can focus on what they do best: creating beautiful, resin-preserved memories.

## 7. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Support Artisans:** Customers are encouraged to engage with Vendors respectfully, leave authentic reviews, and share their feedback to help small businesses thrive.
*   **Care Instructions:** To maintain the longevity of your handcrafted resin art, Customers must follow care guides provided by the Vendor.

## 8. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Artisanal Integrity:** Vendors must maintain high quality standards, package items securely, and represent their products with absolute authenticity.

## 9. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Technical Infrastructure:** We maintain, update, and secure the Platform to ensure a smooth, seamless transaction environment.
*   **Fair Policy Enforcement:** We resolve disputes impartially and enforce quality guidelines across all storefronts.
