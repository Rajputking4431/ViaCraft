# Return Policy

**Effective Date:** [Effective Date]  
**Last Updated:** [Last Updated]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft is a multi-vendor marketplace facilitator specializing in handmade resin products, customized gifts, jewellery, and preservation crafts.

This Return Policy governs the rules, criteria, and procedures under which Customers may request the return of products purchased on [Website URL].

## 2. Purpose
This Return Policy clarifies return eligibility, parameters for damaged items, timeline constraints, and exclusions to protect both our artisan Vendors (who make delicate, customized products) and our Customers.

## 3. Platform Facilitation Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a marketplace platform. All return requests are reviewed by the Platform and fulfilled directly by the Vendor who sold the product. Vendors are responsible for accepting returns and issuing replacements in accordance with this policy.

## 4. Return Eligibility Criteria
To qualify for a return, the product must meet the following criteria:
1.  **Notification Window:** The return request must be raised within **48 hours** of delivery.
2.  **Item Condition:** The item must be unused, unwashed, and in the same condition as received. It must be in its original packaging with all labels, tags, and protective padding intact.
3.  **Proof of Defect:** Customers must upload photos or a continuous unboxing video demonstrating the damage, defect, or incorrect item delivery.

## 5. Non-Returnable Items & Exceptions
Due to the handcrafted, customized, and delicate nature of resin products, the following are strictly **non-returnable**:

| Product Category | Return Policy Status | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Resin Preservation Orders** | Non-Returnable | Made using Customer's physical items (e.g. wedding garlands) which cannot be replaced or re-used. |
| **Customized Resin Products** | Non-Returnable | Items containing custom names, photographs, initials, or unique text. |
| **Personalized Resin Jewellery** | Non-Returnable | Custom sizes, materials, or hygiene-related items (e.g., earrings). |
| **Clearance / Sale Items** | Non-Returnable | Marked down inventory sold under final sale terms. |

## 6. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Prompt Request:** Customers must submit return requests via the Customer Dashboard or by emailing [Support Email] with valid evidence (e.g., photos/video).
*   **Safe Return Shipping:** Once a return is approved, the Customer must pack the product securely to prevent damage in transit. The Platform may arrange a pickup, or the Customer may be asked to ship it back, depending on the Vendor's location.

## 7. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Accurate Descriptions:** Vendors must describe items accurately to prevent returns due to listing discrepancies.
*   **Inspection & Approval:** Vendors have the right to inspect returned items at their workshops. If the item is damaged by the Customer, the return request will be rejected.
*   **Replacement Processing:** If a return is due to Vendor error or shipping damage, the Vendor must immediately process a replacement or authorize a refund.

## 8. Return & Replacement Process Flow
1.  **Request Submission:** Customer initiates the return via the Platform dashboard within 48 hours.
2.  **Review Phase:** The Platform team and Vendor review the unboxing video and photos.
3.  **Approval / Rejection:** If approved, a reverse shipping label is generated.
4.  **Handoff:** The courier partner picks up the item from the Customer.
5.  **Receipt & Verification:** The Vendor receives the product and confirms its original state.
6.  **Resolution:** Vendor ships a replacement or initiates a refund request to the platform.

## 9. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not responsible for transit damage during reverse shipping caused by improper packaging by the Customer. The Platform's liability is strictly confined to facilitating the return communication flow between the Customer and the Vendor.

## 10. Important Notices
> [!WARNING]
> Since resin items are handcrafted, minor variations in color, texture, micro-bubbles, and flower placement are natural characteristics of the craft and do not constitute defects. Such variations are not eligible for returns.
