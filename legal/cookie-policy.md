## 4. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device, remember preferences, and analyze performance.

## 5. Categories of Cookies Used on ViaCraft
We use the following types of cookies:

### 5.1. Essential (Necessary) Cookies
These are required for the basic operation of the Platform. They enable you to log in to your Customer or Vendor dashboard, keep items in your shopping cart, and perform secure checkouts.

### 5.2. Performance & Functional Cookies
These cookies remember your preferences, such as language settings, dark/light mode choices, and sort configurations on the shop page.

### 5.3. Analytics & Statistics Cookies
We use analytics tools to collect information on how visitors interact with the Platform (e.g. page views, click coordinates). This data is anonymized and used for interface optimization.

### 5.4. Marketing & Advertising Cookies
These track your browsing habits to deliver targeted advertisements on social media platforms or other websites.

## 10. How to Manage and Block Cookies
Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. You can modify your settings to block cookies:
*   **Google Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Third-party cookies.
*   **Mozilla Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
*   **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Block all cookies.

## 11. Exceptions & Third-Party Cookies
*   **Payment Gateways:** Standard security cookies used by payment partners during the checkout flow bypass user blocking preferences because they are required to detect credit card fraud.
