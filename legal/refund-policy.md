# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** [Effective Date]  
**Last Updated:** [Last Updated]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft operates as a multi-vendor marketplace facilitator for resin handmade gifts, home decor, jewellery, and preservation products. 

This Refund Policy governs the eligibility criteria, process, and timelines for refund requests made on [Website URL] (the "Platform").

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this Refund Policy is to outline how refunds are processed, the timelines for payment gateways, the treatment of platform fees, and how refunds apply to various types of transactions (e.g. customized resin pieces vs. stock items).

## 3. Platform Facilitation Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a transaction facilitator. Payments collected are held securely and settled to the independent Vendor after deducting the platform facilitation commission (8–10%). Refunds are funded by the respective Vendor, and ViaCraft facilitates the transfer back to the Customer's original payment source.

## 4. Refund Eligibility Criteria
A Customer is eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:
1.  **Damaged/Defective Products:** The product was damaged during shipping, and the Vendor cannot provide a replacement.
2.  **Order Cancellation:** The order was cancelled by the Customer before shipping (subject to cancellation timelines) or cancelled by the Vendor/Admin due to stock unavailability.
3.  **Incorrect Item:** The item received was completely different from what was ordered, and a return has been processed.

## 5. Non-Refundable Categories
Refunds will **not** be issued under these circumstances:
*   **Custom / Preservation Orders:** Completed custom orders, including resin flower preservation blocks, cannot be refunded once manufacturing has begun.
*   **Customer Handling:** Products damaged by the Customer due to improper handling, dropping, exposure to direct sunlight (which yellows resin), or heating.
*   **Minor Craft Variations:** Handcrafted items may have minor imperfections (like tiny bubbles or wood grain variances). These are not eligible for a refund.

## 6. Refund Process & Step-by-Step Flow
To request a refund:
1.  **Submission:** Email [Support Email] or go to [Website URL]/refunds and submit order details, reasons for refund, and unboxing video/photo proof.
2.  **Investigation:** The Platform verifies the dispute with the Vendor.
3.  **Approval/Rejection:** The Platform notifies the Customer within 3 to 5 business days of the refund decision.
4.  **Transaction Trigger:** If approved, the refund is initiated via our payment gateway.

## 7. Refund Timelines & Payment Options
Once a refund is approved, the funds will be credited back to the original source of payment:

| Payment Method | Estimated Processing Timeline |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Credit Card / Debit Card** | 5 to 7 Business Days |
| **UPI / Net Banking** | 2 to 4 Business Days |
| **Mobile Wallets** | 24 to 48 Hours |
| **Store Credit (Optional)** | Instant (added to Customer Account) |

## 8. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Accurate Bank Info:** For Net Banking or manual payout refunds, the Customer must provide accurate bank account details, including account holder name, account number, and IFSC code.
*   **Evidence Submission:** The Customer must cooperate with support by providing photos/videos within the timelines specified in the Return Policy.

## 9. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Refund Liability:** If a refund is approved due to Vendor packaging failure or poor craftsmanship, the refund amount will be deducted from the Vendor's subsequent settlement.
*   **Prompt Resolution:** Vendors must respond to refund disputes raised by the Platform team within 48 hours.

## 10. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Gateway Coordination:** ViaCraft coordinates with payment processors to ensure refunds are handled securely.
*   **Grievance Resolution:** In case of a stalemate between a Vendor and Customer, ViaCraft retains final authority to decide on the refund outcome based on the evidence presented.

## 11. Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued in cases where:
*   A bundle order was purchased, and only a portion of the bundle arrived damaged or was out of stock.
*   Minor repairable damages occurred, and the Customer agreed to keep the product in exchange for a partial refund.

## 12. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not responsible for any banking system delays, payment gateway downtime, or account blockages that may delay the processing of refunds. The Platform's liability is capped at the total amount paid by the Customer for the specific transaction.
