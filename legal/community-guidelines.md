## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft is a multi-vendor online marketplace specializing in handcrafted resin products, customized gifts, home decor, and preservation crafts.

Our Community Guidelines are designed to ensure that [Website URL] remains a safe, respectful, and inspiring environment for Customers, Vendors, and creators alike.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of these guidelines is to outline the standard of behavior expected from everyone interacting on the Platform. This includes rules regarding communication, reviews, listings, and the prevention of fraud, abuse, and harassment.

## 3. Platform Facilitation Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a facilitation platform and does not control the independent actions or personal views of Users. However, the Platform reserves the absolute right to moderate content, suspend accounts, and delete reviews or listings that violate these guidelines.

## 4. Guidelines for Customers
*   **Honest Feedback:** Reviews must be honest, truthful, and based on actual purchase experiences.
*   **Respectful Communication:** When communicating with Vendors regarding customized specifications, Customers must remain polite and professional.
*   **No Extortion:** Customers must not use threats of negative reviews to extort refunds, discounts, or additional free products from Vendors.

## 5. Guidelines for Vendors
*   **Honest Representation:** Product images and descriptions must accurately represent the product. Plagiarism of designs or using other artists' work as listing photos is strictly prohibited.
*   **Fair Competition:** Vendors must not post negative reviews on competitors' pages or engage in price manipulation schemes.
*   **Professionalism:** Vendors must handle Customer queries with patience, respect, and professionalism.

## 6. Prohibited Activities (Zero Tolerance)
The following activities are strictly prohibited on the Platform:

### 6.1. Harassment and Abuse
*   Using profane, hateful, racist, or sexist language in chat messages, reviews, or store descriptions.
*   Doxing or sharing another User's personal details (phone number, address) without permission.

### 6.2. Fraud and Manipulation
*   Creating fake listings to inflate search rankings or reviews.
*   Shill bidding or purchasing your own items to boost transaction counts.
*   Encouraging Customers to pay outside the Platform checkout to avoid platform commissions.

### 6.3. Spam and Off-Topic Content
*   Posting promotional links, advertisements, or spam in product reviews or Q&A sections.
*   Sending unsolicited promotional messages to Customers through the Platform messenger.

## 7. Review and Moderation Policy
*   **Review Removal:** ViaCraft will remove reviews containing profanity, advertising, personally identifiable details, or those verified as fake or malicious.
*   **Reporting:** Users can report inappropriate behavior or listings by emailing [Support Email].

## 8. Exceptions & Special Cases
*   **Public Interest:** The Platform may share logs, messages, and account details with law enforcement agencies in cases of fraud, theft, or explicit illegal activities, bypassing standard account confidentiality.

## 9. Enforcement & Penalties
Violations of these Community Guidelines may result in:
1.  **Warning:** A formal warning issued to the account holder.
2.  **Listing Removal:** Deletion of violating product listings.
3.  **Account Suspension:** Temporary suspension of buying or selling privileges (usually 7 to 30 days).
4.  **Permanent Ban:** Complete deletion of the user profile and blacklist of bank/email addresses.

## 10. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not responsible for user-generated content, reviews, or messages. The user who posts or transmits the content is solely liable for any legal actions, claims, or damages arising from it.
