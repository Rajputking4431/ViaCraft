## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft ("Platform"). ViaCraft is a multi-vendor online marketplace designed to facilitate the sale of handcrafted resin products, custom preservation orders, resin gifts, jewellery, and home decor.

In accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, this Grievance Redressal Policy outlines the mechanism for addressing Customer and Vendor complaints.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this Grievance Redressal Policy is to provide a transparent, structured, and legally compliant framework for the fast resolution of user grievances, disputes, and compliance claims.

## 5. Complaint Redressal Process Flow
To file an official grievance:

### Step 1: Submission
The complainant (Customer or Vendor) must send an email to [Support Email] containing:
1.  **Ticket Details:** Order ID, Vendor Name, or customer profile details.
2.  **Summary:** A clear description of the dispute, complaint, or policy breach.
3.  **Proof:** Supporting files (unboxing video, photos, screenshots, or receipt documents).

### Step 2: Acknowledgment
The Platform will acknowledge receipt of the complaint within **48 hours** and issue a unique Grievance Ticket Number.

### Step 3: Investigation & Mediation
*   Our support team will contact the Vendor or Customer involved to obtain their counter-statement.
*   Relevant data logs from our Supabase servers or payment transaction history will be analyzed.

### Step 4: Resolution
*   The Grievance Officer will issue a final decision and resolution within **15 business days** of receiving the complaint.

## 6. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Accurate Claims:** Customers must submit genuine complaints with clear visual proof. Fabricating disputes to get free products violates our Community Guidelines.
*   **Good Faith:** Complainants must participate in mediation in good faith before taking external legal action.

## 7. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Cooperation:** Vendors must respond to the Grievance Officer's inquiries within 48 hours.
*   **Resolution Honor:** Vendors must accept the final resolution determined by the Grievance Officer (e.g. issuing a refund, sending a replacement).

## 8. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Impartiality:** The Grievance Officer will evaluate cases objectively based on transaction evidence.
*   **Compliance:** The Platform will maintain logs of all complaints and resolutions for statutory review as required by Indian laws.

## 9. Limitation of Liability
The Grievance Redressal Desk's liability is limited to facilitating refunds, replacements, or account reinstatements. ViaCraft is not liable for secondary costs or damages resulting from delayed resolutions.

## 10. Important Notes
> [!WARNING]
> Under the IT Act, any person who knowingly makes false or vexatious complaints to the Grievance Desk may face account suspension, platform blacklisting, or civil legal penalties.
