## 2. Purpose
This Cancellation Policy outlines the timelines, refund rules, and potential cancellation charges to prevent disruptions to our custom-artisan workflows while ensuring fair treatment for Customers.

## 3. Platform Facilitation Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is a marketplace facilitator. All cancellation requests are processed in accordance with these unified rules. Vendors are responsible for updating order statuses (e.g. "Processing", "Shipped") promptly to ensure accurate cancellation processing.

## 4. Cancellation Rules by Category
Due to the distinction between stock items and customized/preservation crafts, different cancellation rules apply:

### 4.1. Stock / Standard Items (Non-Customized)
*   **Customer Cancellation:** Customers can cancel orders for standard products at any time **before** the Vendor packages and marks the order as "Shipped" or "Ready for Pickup".
*   **Refund:** A full refund will be processed back to the original payment source.

### 4.2. Customized & Resin Preservation Orders
*   **Immediate Window:** Cancellations are only permitted within **24 hours** of order placement.
*   **After 24 Hours:** Once the Vendor has purchased customized raw materials or initiated resin curation/preservation workflows, cancellations are **not permitted**, and no refunds will be issued.
*   **Preservation Orders:** If a Customer books a preservation order but fails to send their items (e.g., wedding garland) to the Vendor within 30 days, the order is subject to admin cancellation and may incur a processing fee of up to 30%.

## 5. Cancellation Scenarios & Rules

### 5.1. Customer Cancellation
*   **Before Dispatch:** Allowed via the Customer Dashboard. No penalty applies.
*   **After Dispatch:** Not allowed. The Customer must wait for the package to arrive and request a return, subject to Return Policy terms.

### 5.2. Vendor Cancellation
A Vendor may cancel an order under the following conditions:
*   **Stock Issues:** Raw materials (e.g. specific wood types or premium resin pigments) are unavailable.
*   **Inability to Fulfill:** Custom specifications requested by the Customer cannot be fulfilled.
*   **Damage/Defect:** The pre-made item is found to be defective before packing.
*   **Refund:** Customers will receive a **100% refund** for Vendor-initiated cancellations.

### 5.3. Admin / Platform Cancellation
ViaCraft reserves the right to cancel orders under the following circumstances:
*   **Fraud Detection:** The transaction is flag-marked as high risk or fraudulent by the payment gateway.
*   **Pricing Errors:** The product was listed with an obvious pricing mistake or system glitch.
*   **Non-Responsive Vendor/Customer:** The Vendor does not confirm order processing within 5 business days, or the Customer fails to supply necessary customization inputs.

## 6. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Prompt Request:** Customers must initiate cancellations through the dashboard as soon as possible.
*   **Communication:** Customers must respond to Vendor queries regarding customized layouts to avoid automatic cancellation.

## 7. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Inventory Accuracy:** Vendors must keep their store stock updated to prevent cancellations.
*   **Timely Processing:** Vendors must accept or decline orders within 48 hours. Excessive cancellations by a Vendor may lead to store suspension.

## 8. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Transaction Processing:** ViaCraft will process the cancellation and trigger refund operations through the integrated payment gateway within 2 business days.
*   **Notification:** Automated email notifications will be dispatched to all parties notifying them of the cancellation status.

## 9. Exceptions & Special Cases
*   **Delivery Attempts:** If a courier partner attempts delivery 3 times and the Customer rejects the delivery, the order is cancelled. A refund will be issued after deducting reverse shipping charges and a restocking fee (usually 15-20%, not applicable to custom orders where no refund is allowed).

## 10. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not liable for any disappointment, secondary losses, or delays resulting from cancelled orders. The Platform's maximum liability is limited to returning the payment received from the Customer.
