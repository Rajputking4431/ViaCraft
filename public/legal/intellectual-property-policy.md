## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft operates as a multi-vendor marketplace facilitator for resin handmade gifts, home decor, jewellery, and preservation products.

This Intellectual Property Policy ("IP Policy") outlines the guidelines regarding copyrights, trademarks, design rights, and the procedures for reporting infringement on [Website URL].

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this IP Policy is to protect the intellectual property rights of creators, artisans, and third-party brands on the Platform while maintaining a fair and compliant marketplace.

## 3. Platform Facilitator Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft is an intermediary platform. We do not manufacture or source the products listed on our website. All content in product listings (including photos, descriptions, and designs) is uploaded directly by independent Vendors. ViaCraft is not liable for unauthorized listings but will act promptly to remove infringing material upon receiving a valid report.

## 4. Protected Intellectual Property Categories
The Platform respects the following forms of intellectual property:
*   **Copyrights:** Original text, product descriptions, listing images, photos, and promotional banners.
*   **Trademarks:** Brand names, logos, slogans, and distinctive product names.
*   **Design Rights:** Unique, proprietary shapes, mold patterns, and configurations of resin and wood creations.

## 5. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Original Content:** Vendors must only upload images, text, and logos they own, have created themselves, or have written authorization to use.
*   **Design Authenticity:** Vendors must not replicate trademarked characters (e.g. Disney, Marvel) or copy other artisans' proprietary designs without permission.
*   **Consequences of Infringement:** If a Vendor is found to have violated third-party IP rights, their listings will be removed, and their store may be suspended.

## 6. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Personal Use:** Customers purchasing custom resin creations (e.g., preservation items) must respect the designer's copyrights and not reproduce the items for commercial sale without consent.
*   **Customization Inputs:** Customers providing images or logos for customization must ensure they possess the legal right to reproduce that imagery.

## 7. Infringement Reporting Procedure (Takedown Notice)
If you believe that a listing on ViaCraft violates your intellectual property rights, you can file a Takedown Notice by emailing [Support Email] with the following information:
1.  **Identification:** Details of the copyrighted work, trademark, or design claimed to have been infringed.
2.  **Location:** The exact URL(s) of the infringing listing(s) on the Platform.
3.  **Contact Info:** Your name, address, telephone number, and email.
4.  **Statement of Good Faith:** A statement that you believe in good faith that the use of the material is not authorized by the owner, its agent, or the law.
5.  **Signature:** Physical or electronic signature of the IP owner or authorized representative.

## 8. Counter-Notice Procedure
If a Vendor believes their listing was removed due to a mistake or misidentification, they may submit a Counter-Notice containing:
1.  **Identification:** The material that was removed and the URL before removal.
2.  **Contact Info:** Vendor's name, address, email, and phone number.
3.  **Consent to Jurisdiction:** A statement consenting to the jurisdiction of courts in [City/State, India].
4.  **Signature:** Physical or electronic signature of the Vendor.

## 9. Exceptions & Special Cases
*   **Fair Use:** Certain references to brand names may be permitted if used descriptively (e.g., "Fits iPhone 14" for a phone case) and not in a way that suggests endorsement or affiliation.

## 10. Platform Actions & Repeat Infringers
*   **Expedited Removal:** Upon receipt of a valid IP infringement notice, ViaCraft will remove or disable access to the disputed material within 36 hours.
*   **Repeat Infringer Policy:** Vendors who receive three or more valid infringement notices will be permanently banned from the Platform.

## 11. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft shall not be liable to any party for damages (direct or indirect) resulting from the removal of content or listings in response to a good-faith IP infringement notice, or for the Vendor's initial infringement.
