## 5. Customer Responsibilities
*   **Complete Information:** When contacting support, Customers must state their Order ID, transaction reference, and registered email address to expedite resolutions.
*   **Professional Behavior:** Support requests containing abusive, threatening, or profane language will be blocked.

## 6. Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Timely Updates:** Vendors must respond to customer support requests escalated by the Platform team within 24 hours.

## 7. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Resolution Standards:** General support queries are typically resolved within **24 to 48 hours**. Grievances escalated to the Grievance Desk will be acknowledged within 48 hours and resolved within 15 business days.

## 8. Exceptions & Special Cases
*   **Emergency Contact:** During severe system outages or security issues, support requests will be prioritized. Response times may be extended during natural disasters or public holidays.

## 9. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not responsible for delayed responses caused by spelling errors in email submissions, spam filter blockage, or internet network failure.
