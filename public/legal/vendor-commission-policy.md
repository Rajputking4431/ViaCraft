## 1. Introduction
Welcome to ViaCraft. ViaCraft is a multi-vendor online marketplace facilitating transactions between independent artisans ("Vendors") and buyers ("Customers") for handmade resin products, home decor, customized gifts, and preservation crafts.

This Vendor Commission Policy outlines the platform commission rates, fee calculations, settlement cycles, tax deductions, and financial adjustments for cancelled or returned orders.

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish clear and transparent rules regarding how commissions are calculated, how settlements are processed, and the financial obligations of registered Vendors.

## 3. Platform Facilitation Status
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Facilitator Disclaimer:** ViaCraft operates strictly as a facilitation marketplace. The commission collected is utilized to maintain the Platform infrastructure, manage Supabase database servers, handle Cloudinary media storage, process payment gateway charges, and provide customer support services.

## 4. Commission Structure
ViaCraft charges a standard platform facilitation commission on all sales:
*   **Commission Rate:** **8% to 10%** of the product listing price (excluding shipping fees, depending on the Vendor tier or product category).
*   **Default Commission Rate:** **10%** unless otherwise negotiated or specified in a promotional Vendor agreement.

## 5. Commission Calculation Methodology
Commissions are calculated on the gross product price set by the Vendor.

### 5.1. Fee Breakdown Example:
*   **Vendor Product Price:** ₹2,000 (inclusive of GST, if applicable)
*   **Platform Commission (10%):** ₹200
*   **GST on Commission (18% of Commission):** ₹36 (Subject to Indian tax rules on service fees)
*   **Payment Gateway Charges (e.g. 2%):** ₹40 (Included in platform commission or added separately depending on gateway tier)
*   **Net Payout to Vendor:** ₹1,764 (Assuming shipping is managed directly by the Vendor)

## 6. Settlement Cycle & Payouts
Settlement funds are disbursed to the Vendor's registered bank account in accordance with the following rules:
*   **Settlement Trigger:** Settlements are processed once the order status is updated to "Delivered" and the Customer return window (48 hours) has closed without dispute.
*   **Disbursement Schedule:** Payouts are batch-processed weekly on **every [Weekday/Wednesday]** for all completed orders from the previous cycle.
*   **Minimum Payout Threshold:** The minimum balance for settlement is ₹500. Balances below this amount will accumulate until the threshold is met.

## 7. Taxation & Compliance (India)
As a marketplace operating in India, ViaCraft complies with the Income Tax Act, 1961, and GST laws:
*   **Tax Collection at Source (TCS):** Under Section 52 of the CGST Act, ViaCraft is required to collect TCS at the rate of **1%** (0.5% CGST + 0.5% SGST, or 1% IGST) on the net value of taxable supplies made through the Platform by registered Vendors.
*   **Tax Deducted at Source (TDS):** Under Section 194O of the Income Tax Act, ViaCraft will deduct TDS at the rate of **1%** (or 5% if PAN is not provided) on the gross amount of sales facilitated through the Platform.

## 8. Cancellation, Return, & Failed Order Adjustments

| Transaction Scenario | Commission Status | Payout Adjustment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cancelled before Shipping** | Refunded | No commission charged; payment gateway fees may apply. |
| **Vendor Cancellation** | Refunded | No commission charged; Vendor may be charged processing fees if cancellation rate exceeds threshold. |
| **Customer Return (Approved)** | Refunded | Commission is credited back to the Vendor; however, return shipping costs are deducted from the Vendor. |
| **Failed Delivery (RTO)** | Restocked | Reverse shipping charges are applied to the Vendor; no commission is retained. |

## 9. Customer & Vendor Responsibilities
*   **Vendor Billing:** The Vendor is responsible for invoicing the Customer for the full product price and paying the applicable product GST.
*   **Customer Transactions:** Customers must pay via Platform-approved payment methods to ensure transactions are tracked and settled correctly.

## 10. Platform Responsibilities
*   **Accurate Reporting:** ViaCraft provides detailed sales reports, tax invoices for commission fees, and settlement summaries in the Vendor Dashboard.
*   **Timely Settlement:** ViaCraft will make reasonable efforts to disburse funds according to the weekly schedule, except during public banking holidays.

## 11. Limitation of Liability
ViaCraft is not liable for delayed payouts caused by banking failures, incorrect bank account details provided by the Vendor, or government-mandated payment freezes.
